Who Are We?

Neon Alley is a celebration of the golden era of arcade games, the time when arcades first arose from the backs of bowling alleys and bars and made a name for themselves. Run by an experienced, arcade-game-loving team, we are fully prepared to cater to all of your private, corporate, and casual needs. Come along and enjoy the 50+ arcade machines and handcrafted menu, all whilst basking in the atmosphere under the neon lights.

Covid-19 Info

Our numbers are limited to 50 due to Covid-19 regulations. Arcade machines, and anything else commonly touched, is being cleaned every half-hour. Please remember to keep two meters distance between you and others, and wear a face covering if possible.
